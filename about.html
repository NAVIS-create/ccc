Our History

Decades of dominance in Sri Lankan cricket.

The Legacy

1892 - The Foundation

Established as an exclusive sports club for cricket enthusiasts, paving the way for structured tournaments in the nation.

1930s - Golden Era Beginnings

Entered the top-flight domestic league, securing back-to-back major regional championships.

1981 - International Recognition

Produced highly skilled players who represented the national side during their inaugural Test match.

2020s - Modern Dominance

Reconstructed world-class training facilities and clinched the Major Club T20 Championship.

Our Vision

To be the driving force behind Sri Lanka's international cricket resurgence, fostering an environment where elite talent is nurtured, and sporting excellence is celebrated on a global stage.

Our Mission

To provide cutting-edge facilities, unparalleled coaching, and competitive pathways for athletes while upholding the rich traditions and integrity of the gentleman's game.

The premier cricket club in Sri Lanka. Forging international champions since 1892.
